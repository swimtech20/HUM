An application for all your house management needs.

Main Functions

Get Started Quickly

Join or create a house group to cater to your house management needs.

Chores

Add chores and assign them to any member of your house. Chores are things that needs to be done around the house on a regular basis, such as taking out the garbage or vacuuming.

Tasks

Add tasks to the house to be done by any memeber of your household. A task is something that needs to be every once in a while, such as cleaning the gutters or mulching the yard.

Events

Let your roommates know when you would like to reserve a room for an event article at a specific time and date.

Laundry Machines

Let your housemates know when you want to do laundry by reserving the machines for a specific time and date.

Easy to create and customize.

Sign up with your email and create username and a password. You can create a group for your house, apartment, or whatever living situation you are in. Enter in the emails for those you wish to invite. That's it! You are now ready to customize our app to fit your needs. Happy HUMming.
